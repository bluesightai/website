Insight

Problem

Machine learning for satellite data today is as fragmented as natural language processing was back in 2018. Slightly changing the task (e.g. regression vs. segmentation) often requires retraining and debugging models from scratch. Additionally, scaling to new regions incurs high costs due to the need for extensive ground-truth data collection.

Solution

We believe these challenges can be addressed through the productization of geo-spatial foundational models. Our current offerings include hosted inference for the open-source Clay model and a fine-tuning API for popular downstream tasks. We are collaborating with geo-spatial developers to integrate our platform into their workflows and providing end-customers with direct access to actionable intelligence. Get in touch if you have any questions
