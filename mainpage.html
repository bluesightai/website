Platform

Bluesight is an AI platform for geospatial data. We're building it to democratize access to Earth observation insights and empower decision-makers with actionable intelligence about our planet.

Our platform leverages state-of-the-art machine learning models, including the Clay foundation model, to analyze satellite imagery and other geospatial data sources. This allows us to provide valuable insights for various applications such as environmental monitoring, urban planning, agriculture, and disaster response.

By combining cutting-edge AI technology with vast amounts of Earth observation data, Bluesight aims to unlock new possibilities for understanding and managing our planet's resources and challenges.
